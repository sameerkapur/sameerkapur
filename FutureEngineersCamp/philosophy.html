OUR PHILOSOPHY

We teach all students to become great makers



Robots are Fun

If you like building things, engineering may be a great field for you, and Lego is a fun, easy way to learn it. Lego offers a variety of possibilities that makes building complex models easier. This allows you to design and build complex robots without having the technical knowledge required to build a real robot. With the right materials, mindset, and help when you need it, you’ll be able to build an amazing robot after only one week.

Our Passion

Through our personal passion for building with Lego, we’ve found it invaluable to understand and apply mechanical engineering principles. We believe that an early start to building robots can lead to a bright future in engineering. Now we want to share our passion with other kids and help them become great builders, all while having tons of fun. We want everyone to have an opportunity to learn and build robots, and we believe our camp will be a great place to do so. Our teachers follow a student-centered approach to learning. That’s why we keep a 1:3 teacher to student ratio.

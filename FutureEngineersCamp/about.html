Our Teachers





 

Aashish Kapur

Aashish Kapur, a senior at Monta Vista High School, is a build leader on the Monta Vista Robotics Team, where he and other members are responsible for building a robot to compete in the First Robotics Competition challenge. He is a two-time award-winning FLL coach and two year participant in FLL, where he gained knowledge with the LEGO NXT system.



 

Tyler Smithline

Tyler is a four-time First Lego League® participant who has helped his team win three champion’s awards. He is the author of halfrobot.blogspot.com and is the youngest member of the Cupertino Teen Commission. He loves helping kids learn and is excited to share his passion for engineering with lego.



 

Sameer Kapur

Sameer is a three-time award-winning FIRST LEGO League® participant and experienced young entrepreneur. He started his first successful business while in middle school, and is a key contributor for business development and marketing at Future Engineers Summer Camp.
